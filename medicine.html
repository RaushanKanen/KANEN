Medicine in Kazakhstan

Medicine in Kazakhstan, like in any other country, is designed to protect the health and well-being of citizens of Kazakhstan. The health care system of Kazakhstan is currently at the stage of transition to market relations. In recent years, the government has taken a number of measures aimed at reforming and developing health care. Currently, the Government of the Republic of Kazakhstan pays close attention to the treatment of tuberculosis, diabetes, AIDS, cancer, as well as the problems of iodine deficiency. Relevant government programs have been adopted in all these areas.
